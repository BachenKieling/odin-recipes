Vegan Lasagna

Classic lasagna al forno with veggie bolognese and béchamel sauce according to mom’s traditional Italian recipe, but made vegan without meat!

This meat-less lasagna is quick and easy to make ahead and tastes incredibly delicious!

Ingedients

  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g vegan ground beef
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1/2 cup red win
  • 400g tomatoes
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 1tbsp italian herbs
