Green Goddess Dressing - Tahini Dip Sauce

This healthy Green Goddess Tahini Dressing recipe is a delicious green herb dressing that is perfect as a sauce for Buddha Bowls, fresh salads, and as a dip for veggies and finger food snacks!

It’s vegan, gluten-free, nut-free and takes less than 5 minutes to make!
