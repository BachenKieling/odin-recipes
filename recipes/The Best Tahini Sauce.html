The Best Tahini Sauce – Easy Vegan Dressing

This is a quick and easy 3-minute recipe for the best vegan tahini sauce that can be used as a dip, salad dressing or dairy-free mayonnaise for veggies, bowls, falafel, tacos and many dishes!

It’s creamy, healthy, very delicious and perfect to make ahead using simple ingredients!
